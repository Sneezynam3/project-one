1961 Chevrolet Bel Air

Chevrolet produced seven generations of the Bel Air. The full size veichle was made in the U.S. from 1950 to 1975. The fifth generation ran from 1961-1964. Although the generation was considered the same through that time period, the designs featured contrasting sheetmetal between the years.

This is my Bel Air. There are others like it. Some better. Some worse. But this one is mine. Here is the story of my Bel Air.

The car was purchased by my father 12-27-2009. Along with a donor car. The donor car was also a 1961, but a Biscayne. The Biscayne is the cheaper trim model of the Bel Air. From there the cars were placed along side each other in a backyard. Due to the ill repair of the two, cutting, welding, and fabricating was required to combine them into one.

My father unfortunately passed away 7-2-2017. I have taken on the project and have further refined the idea of this beautifly crafted automobile. Here is the car after sitting about a year in my garage.
