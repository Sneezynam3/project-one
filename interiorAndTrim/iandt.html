Interior

Similarly to how the sheet metal has minute differences between fifth generation years, the interiors have personalities as well. While some parts were a direct share beetween the 61-62 years, I have elected to go with the 63-64 bucket seats. The 61-62 stock versions shared a bench seat for the front.

In the rear of the car the speaker grill is different.

The antenna bears a tear drop shape for the '61.
